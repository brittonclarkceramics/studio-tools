Studio Tools Suite
Welcome to Price It Right

A pricing calculator built for ceramic makers — no accounting background needed.

1
Set your studio costs at the top Enter your kiln firing cost, studio overhead (rent, tools), and packaging costs as monthly totals. The app divides them across your pieces automatically.
2
Add your products Use the + tab to add each item you make — mugs, bowls, vases. Enter your material costs and how long each piece takes you.
3
See your suggested price The app calculates what you should charge to cover all your costs and pay yourself fairly. Drag the slider to explore what happens at different price points.
4
Plan your production mix Head to Line Summary to see how selling a mix of products gets you to your monthly revenue goal — without needing to sell 96 mugs.
Reset everything?

This will clear all your products, costs, and settings and return the app to its default state. This can't be undone.

Step 1 of 7
💰 Hourly Rate
What you want to pay yourself per hour of work. This gets factored into the cost of every piece you make — enter what feels fair for your skill and time.
🎯 Monthly Goal
How much total revenue you want to bring in each month from your ceramics. Used to calculate how many pieces you'd need to sell to hit your target.
🏺 Pieces / Month
How many pieces you typically make in a month across all product types. Used to spread your fixed studio costs — rent, kiln, packaging — evenly across your output.
