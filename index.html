Built by a maker, for makers

These tools were built out of a genuine frustration with spreadsheets and guesswork. As a ceramic artist and project manager, I kept noticing that makers — myself included — were underpricing their work, losing track of production, and leaving money on the table at markets.

The Studio Tools Suite is my attempt to fix that with something simple, honest, and actually useful in the studio and at the booth. No accounting background required.

— Britton Clark
